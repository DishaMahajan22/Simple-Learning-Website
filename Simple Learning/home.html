What do we teach?

Our extensive knowledge in the day-to-day school subjects has allowed us to create this program for your child. About Us

We are high school students working together to make available the opportunity of free summer help for your elementary age child. This program will help prepare your child for the upcoming school year by providing simplified and easy to learn concepts that solidify previous learning concepts, and start to build a foundation for new concepts in the upcoming year. So sign up for this free program and have access to English, Math, History, and Science courses for your child.
